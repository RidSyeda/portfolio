Sitemap

A site map visually represents a website's structure, showing pages and their hierarchy. It helps designers and developers plan layout and navigation, aiding users in finding information easily. It's made interactive, accessible, and responsive, seamlessly integrating into the website design.The sitemap was developed by the help of SVG (scalar vector graphics) it enables users to see the links between each webpage and gives an overall depiction of the website.
